Welcome

Found in the southwest Pacific Ocean, this island is famous for its incredible scenery. Ranging from huge glaciers to boiling hot springs, golden-sand beaches and a rugged coastline this Country offers much diversity.In addition to the beautiful scenery, New Zealand also has a vast underground cave system and other neat hidden gems found within the city. Many films were shot in New Zealand thanks to its magnificent Landscape.

History

Dating back 700 years ago, this Country was first discovered by Polynesians who developed the Maori culture which was centered around connection to the land. In 1642, the dutch explorer Abel Tasman arrived and called the country Nieuw Zeeland.
